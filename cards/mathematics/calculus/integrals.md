Q: What is the integral of $e^x$?
A: $\int e^x dx = e^x + C$

---

Q: What is the integral of $\cos(x)$?
A: $\int \cos(x) dx = \sin(x) + C$

---

Q: What is the integral of $\sin(x)$?
A: $\int \sin(x) dx = -\cos(x) + C$

---

Q: What is the integral of $\ln(x)$?
A: $\int \ln(x) dx = x\ln(x) - x + C$

---

Q: What is the integral of $\frac{1}{x}$?
A: $\int \frac{1}{x} dx = \ln|x| + C$

---

---

Q: What is the integral of $x^n$ (where $n \neq -1$)?
A: $\int x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C$
